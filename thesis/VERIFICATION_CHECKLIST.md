## If You Find Issues

### If article numbers are wrong:
1. Correct them in the LaTeX files
2. Recompile to ensure references update
3. Double-check surrounding context still makes sense

### If paraphrasing is too close:
1. Use the "Action if too similar" suggestions above
2. Or rewrite in your own words completely
3. Maintain the citation

### If you're unsure:
- **Conservative approach:** Rewrite to be safer
- **When in doubt:** Ask your thesis advisor to review that specific passage
